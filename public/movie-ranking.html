The Best Military Movies According to myself (Abram)

My top 3 Military movies of all-time.

Black Hawk Down

This movie depicts a U.S. military raid in Somalia that turns into a fierce battle for survival. It's known for its realistic portrayal of close-quarters combat and the chaos of war.

Saving Private Ryan

A classic war film that follows a group of U.S. soldiers on a mission to find and retrieve a paratrooper whose brothers have all been killed in action. The film is famous for its intense and graphic depiction of the D-Day landings.

Fury

Set during the final days of World War II, Fury follows a Sherman tank crew as they face overwhelming odds in Germany. The movie provides a gritty and realistic look at armored warfare and the psychological toll of combat.
